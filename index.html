About

Daniel Gologorsky, M.D., M.B.A.

Daniel Gologorsky, MD, MBA is a compassionate Ophthalmologist and fellowship-trained Vitreoretinal specialist who takes care of patients with diabetic retinopathy, macular degeneration, retinal detachment, and other eye pathology. Please contact our office with any questions.
